# Agentic AI-Powered Business Analyst

Agentic AI-Powered Business Analyst is a **Streamlit web app** that acts as a virtual business analyst using **Google Gemini** via **LangChain**. It automates data analysis, insight generation, and report creation to help business users make informed decisions.

## Capabilities

- **Goal-to-Plan Conversion**: Converts user-defined business goals into structured, actionable plans.
- **Data Analysis**: Performs analysis on uploaded datasets to extract meaningful metrics and patterns.
- **Insight Generation**: Generates concise, actionable business insights highlighting trends, anomalies, and opportunities.
- **Interactive Chat**: Provides a chat interface to discuss data, analysis, and insights with AI context awareness.
- **Report Generation**: Creates professional business reports with customizable sections and exports them as PDF.
- **Context-Aware**: Maintains context across planning, analysis, insights, chat, and report generation.

## Quick Start

1. Upload your dataset and define a business goal.
2. Generate a structured plan and perform analysis.
3. View insights or interact via the chat interface.
4. Generate a report based on the analysis and insights.

Streamline your business decision-making with AI-powered automation.
